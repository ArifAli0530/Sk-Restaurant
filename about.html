About Us

SK Restaurant is fast food restaurant website for fast food lovers. Anyone can order any fast food from this website through the restaurant. Foods like: pizza, burger, sandwich, noodles etc. They can also book a table in the restaurant by sitting at home. The main objective of this project is to provide fast food to everyone who is so fond of fast food and deliver food to people who are not able to come at our restaurant. We also provide a table booking option in website for book a table by customer in restaurant.
